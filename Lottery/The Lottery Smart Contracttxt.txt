Lottery Smart Contract
1.The lottery starts by accepting ETH transactions. Anyone having an Ethererum waller can send a fixed amount of 0.1 ETH to the contract's address.
2.The playes send ETH directly to the contract address and tehir Ethereum address is registered. A user can send more transactions having more chances to win.
3.There is a manager, account that deploys and controls the contract.
4.At some point, if there are at least 3 players, he can pick a random winner from the players list. Only the manager is allowed to see the contract balance
  and to randomly select the winner.
5.The contract will transfer the entire balance to the winner's address and the lotttery is reset and ready for the next round.
